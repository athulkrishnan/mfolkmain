Our Philosophy

Here at MouseFolk, we believe in the inherent power of the paintbrush, that intricate musical note, and the written word. Being artists, we know the joy of bringing fragile, evolving ideas of the mind into visual, tactile and shareable forms.

The joy of creativity is unlike anything else. The path to creativity though, is quite demanding. We're here to make it a little simpler for artists like you. We'll ensure that your creativity is appreciated by the world - as much as the joy you had in creating it. Our vision is to make the artistic experience a little less painful, a bit more rewarding, and a lot more fulfilling. You keep making great art, we’ll make sure you find the right audience.

Our name is inspired by Franz Kafka's final short story - “Josephine the Singer, or The Mouse Folk”. We identified with it, for it narrates the plight of a singer, who happens to be a mouse. It best describes the artistic experience of standing by your vision even when predicaments say otherwise.

Frequently Asked Questions:
Where will my artwork be used?
You have full control over how your artwork will be used. Your work can be provided to buyers as printed t-shirts, posters, or as digital downloads.
What about copyrights?
You retain all rights over your work, we only license them.
How do I get rewarded?
You'll be paid for sales within 4-6 days. Cheque, bank transfer or online wallets - you name it.
What are the membership charges?
None. We charge a commision of 20-40 percent on each sale, excluding the printing and shipping charges.
